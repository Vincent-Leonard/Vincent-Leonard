Music

I played music since the age of 6. My very first instrument was a violin. It is a part of my daily activities. Then I started playing the piano when I was at the age of 9. This is how I learn more about music composition. Music has let me meet people who become one of my closest group of friends. It certainly has a huge impact in my life.

There are 4 major musical eras that people play until now which is Baroque, Classical, Romantic, and Modern or 20th Century. Every era has its own musical style and characteristics. Baroque music tends to be repetitive and represent only a single mood (if a song starts happy it will remain happy). Classical music sounds more simple than Baroque but have more dynamic range. Romantic tends to be more expressive and more modulation (change in key). Modern is more dissonant with huge variety of styles that sometimes contains complex harmony.
